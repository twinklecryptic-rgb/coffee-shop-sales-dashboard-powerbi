# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze coffee shop sales performance across different products, stores, and time periods.

The dashboard provides a clear overview of business performance, highlights top-performing products, and identifies key revenue trends to support data-driven decision making.

---

## Features / Highlights

### Business Problem

The coffee shop lacks clear visibility into its sales performance across products, stores, and time periods. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify top-performing products and evaluate store-level performance.

### Goal of the Dashboard

The goal of this dashboard is to provide a comprehensive view of sales performance and product demand. It helps track revenue trends, identify top-selling products, and compare store performance to support better business decisions.

### Walkthrough of Key Visuals

**KPI Cards**
Total Revenue, Monthly Revenue Growth, Total Transactions, Average Order Value, and Total Quantity Sold provide a quick overview of overall business performance.

**Revenue Trend Over Time**
A line chart showing monthly revenue trends to identify sales growth and performance patterns over time.

**Top 10 Best-Selling Products by Revenue**
Highlights the products contributing the most to overall revenue and helps identify key revenue drivers.

**Store Performance Analysis**
Compares revenue across different store locations to identify the highest-performing stores.

**Product Category Performance**
Shows how different product categories contribute to overall sales.

**Interactive Features**
Slicers allow users to filter the dashboard by month, store, and product category for deeper analysis.

**Drill-Through Analysis**
Users can drill into detailed views for deeper product-level insights.

**Custom Tooltips**
Additional contextual insights appear when hovering over visuals, allowing users to explore data without cluttering the dashboard.
